EVERFINDR - Brief Explanation
What is EverFindr?
EverFindr is a device tracking system that helps you recover stolen laptops and gadgets even after thieves wipe and reinstall the operating system.

The Problem It Solves
Traditional tracking apps (like Find My Device) get deleted when the thief wipes the computer

Once deleted, you can't track the device anymore

IP addresses change, making simple IP tracking useless

How EverFindr Is Different
Before Theft:
You install EverFindr once on your device

It captures permanent hardware IDs that NEVER change:

MAC Address (network card - can't be changed)

Motherboard Serial Number

BIOS UUID

Drive Serials

After Theft (Even After Wipe):
Thief wipes hard drive, installs new OS - EverFindr software is gone

But the hardware IDs remain (they're built into the device)

When device connects to internet, ISP sees the MAC address

EverFindr matches it with stolen database

Owner gets silent alert with location

Police get evidence to recover device

Key Advantage
Once registered, always traceable - because you're tracking the hardware, not software that can be deleted.

Simple Analogy
Think of it like a permanent DNA marker on your device. Thieves can change its clothes (software), but they can't change its DNA (hardware).
